At the San Diego Humane Society, we’re more than just a shelter for dogs.

We’re a community that celebrates the unique personalities of every furry friend that comes through our doors.

We know that every dog is special. They’re loyal, loving, and endlessly entertaining.

Whether they’re playful or cuddly, shy or outgoing, each dog has something to offer, and it’s our job to help them shine.

We want to celebrate and show the world what makes each dog so amazing.

We believe that when people meet our dogs, they’ll fall in love and give them a home, or do what they can to support our mission.

So, come join us at our Walk for Animals.

With your help, we can find forever homes for these deserving dogs, one personality at a time.
