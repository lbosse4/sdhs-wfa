Become a Pack Leader

Pack Leaders are an exclusive group of participants who raise $1,000 or more for the Walk for Animals! These top dogs are celebrated with special event perks, including:

•VIP parking.
• Recognition from the stage during the event.
• Recognition in Facebook posts promoting the Walk.
• Walk for Animals 2023 pin.
• Pack Leader T-shirt.

Prizes

As a token of our appreciation for your amazing fundraising efforts, you will be able to choose from a selection of prizes based on your fundraising total. You'll receive an email after the event with instructions for redeeming your prizes, which will then be delivered to the address you provided during registration.

Fundraising Tips

Take a look at our latest blog post! It has a comprehensive guide to help you fundraise and reach your goal! It is filled with easy ideas and helpful tips. We also created a Participant Center intro video (coming soon) to help you navigate all the fun fundraising features once you register!

Team Success

By starting a team for the Walk for Animals, you'll connect and have fun with other animal lovers who support you and San Diego Humane Society! Teams can be made up of your friends, family, colleagues, neighbors or members of any other network you're in. Companies often start teams to build camaraderie among co-workers and their family and friends!
